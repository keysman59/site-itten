«Echinacea» ITTENSY® lozenges are recommended as a supplemental source of vitamin C and menthol, which help you get better more quickly. The echinacea in our lozenges reduces the likelihood of developing acute respiratory infections of viral origin by 58% and shortens the duration of colds by 1.4 days11.

Our dietary supplement lozenges are easy to take anywhere. And they are delicious, healthy, and breath-freshening, when you need to be in your best shape even during a cold.

Intended Use
These lozenges are recommended as a dietary supplement, a source of vitamin C and menthol
Ingredients
Echinacea purpurea herbal extract, peppermint extract, lemon balm extract, sodium ascorbate, citric acid monohydrate (E330) (as an acidulant), maltodextrin, sucralose (E955) (as a sweetener), caramel color (E150a), sucrose, liquid glucose, honey flavor, menthol oil, lemon flavor, purified water
Daily Dosage
The recommended daily dose of «Echinacea» ITTENSY® lozenges for adults is 7.5 g (3 lozenges)

Directions
Adults: 1 lozenge three times a day after meals, in the earlier part of the day. Allow lozenges to dissolve, do not chew or drink water. Duration of use — 7 days
Contraindications
Individual sensitivity to ingredients, pregnancy, breastfeeding, carbohydrate metabolism disorders, diabetes mellitus, excess body weight, progressive systemic diseases (collagenosis, leukemia, multiple sclerosis, tuberculosis)
