«Glycyrrhiza sugar free» ITTENSY® lozenges are recommended as an additional source of vitamin C and glycyrrhizin, which help your body fight viruses and bacteria. This applies both to colds and to inflammatory diseases of the genitourinary system.

The echinacea in our lozenges reduces the likelihood of developing acute respiratory infections of viral origin by 58% and shortens the duration of colds by 1.4 days13. It’s also used to treat cystitis and prostatitis. And since these lozenges are sugar-free, they’re nothing but pure goodness.

Intended Use
These lozenges are recommended as a dietary supplement, a source of vitamin C and glycyrrhizin

Turmeric extract, echinacea purpurea extract, amla fruit extract, licorice root extract, sodium ascorbate, sucralose (E955) and isomalt (E953) (as sweeteners), glycerol (E422) (to preserve moisture), anhydrous citric acid (E330) (as an acidulant), peppermint flavor, lemon oil, beta-carotene color (E160a), purified water

Directions
Adults: 1 lozenge three times a day with meals, in the earlier part of the day. Allow lozenges to dissolve, do not chew or drink water. Duration of use — 7 days
Contraindications
Individual sensitivity to ingredients, pregnancy, breastfeeding, carbohydrate metabolism disorders, diabetes mellitus, excess body weight, progressive systemic diseases (collagenosis, leukemia, multiple sclerosis, tuberculosis). Use with caution if prone to high blood pressure
