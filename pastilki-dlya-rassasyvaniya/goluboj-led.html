Intended Use
These lozenges are recommended as a dietary supplement, a source of menthol
Ingredients
Sucrose, glucose syrup, maltitol syrup (E965) (as a sweetener), glycerol (E422), peppermint oil, natural mint flavor, eucalyptus oil, carrageenan (E407) and pectin (E440) (as thickeners), lecithin (E322) (as an antioxidant), sucralose (E955) (as a sweetener), brilliant blue FCF (E133)
Daily Dosage
The recommended daily dose of «Ice blue» ITTENSY® lozenges for adults is 9 mg (3 lozenges)

Directions
Adults: 1 lozenge three times a day after meals, in the earlier part of the day. Allow lozenges to dissolve, do not chew or drink water. Duration of use — 14 days
Contraindications
Individual sensitivity to ingredients, pregnancy, breastfeeding, carbohydrate metabolism disorders, diabetes mellitus, excess body weight
