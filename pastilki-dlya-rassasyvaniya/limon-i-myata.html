Our lozenges taste just like tea, but they’re not hot, so you don’t have to wait for them to cool down: just grab one and enjoy right away.

Intended Use
These lozenges are recommended as a dietary supplement, a source of vitamin C, menthol, and lemon
Ingredients
Isomalt (E953), lemon balm extract, sodium ascorbate, peppermint extract, citric acid monohydrate (E330), menthol oil, lemon oil, sucralose (E955), beta-carotene color (E160a), peppermint flavor, purified water
Daily Dosage
