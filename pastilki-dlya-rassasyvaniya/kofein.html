Caffeine increases the rate of fat burning

«Caffeine coffee flavor» ITTENSY® lozenges are recommended as a supplemental source of caffeine. Studies have shown that caffeine increases the rate of fat burning by 10% in heavier people and by 29% in leaner people8.

Caffeine is found not just in coffee, but also in other plants, like tea, yerba mate, guarana, cocoa, and cola, and it can also be synthesized.

Intended Use
These lozenges are recommended as a dietary supplement, a source of caffeine
Ingredients
Sucrose, glucose syrup, green tea extract, coffee flavor (carriers: E1520 and E1518; E636 (as a flavor enhancer), methylcyclopentenolone, ethylvanillin, ethylcyclopentenolone, diacetyl, butyric acid), caramel color (E150a), sucralose (E955), purified water
Daily Dosage
The recommended daily dose of «Caffeine coffee flavor» ITTENSY® lozenges for adults is 7.5 g (3 lozenges)

Directions
Adults: 1 lozenge three times a day after meals, in the earlier part of the day. Allow lozenges to dissolve, do not chew or drink water. Duration of use — 14 days
Contraindications
Individual sensitivity to ingredients, pregnancy, breastfeeding, carbohydrate metabolism disorders, diabetes mellitus, excess body weight, increased nervous excitability, insomnia, cardiovascular diseases, heart rhythm disorders, the intake of the lozenges in the evening
