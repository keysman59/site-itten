ITTENSY® is a new, fast-growing brand of dietary supplement lozenges

We recommend our products as sources of menthol, vitamin C, zinc, caffeine, lemon, glucuronic acid and glycyrrhizin. They contain nothing but herbal ingredients and are designed to aid your body in all kinds of circumstances.

ITTENSY® was created by JB, the world’s leading lozenge manufacturer. JB’s come a long way since 1976: today, it’s one of the most innovative and fastest-growing pharmaceutical companies in India.

Thanks to our vast experience in lozenges, we are able to bring innovations into our production to ensure our customers’ healthy future

JB is known both in India and all around the world for its high-quality but affordable products, and also for its unique pharmaceutical labs and production sites.

Our ultramodern automated production in Daman (India) is equipped with high-performance German technology that weighs raw materials before manufacturing, and then mixes, flavors, cools, and shapes them into lozenges.

Our manufacturing facility is in full compliance with current GMP modular-design standards and is capable of producing a huge number of lozenges of various types, shapes, and flavors. We also use the newest and most convenient types of packaging for our lozenges, including blister packs, strips, pillow-packing, and wraps.

A first-class recipe, technology compliance, and strict quality control
These are the foundations that allowed JB to become the leader in manufacture and supply of medicated and herbal lozenges.
